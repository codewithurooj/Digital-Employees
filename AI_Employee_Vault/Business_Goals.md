# Business Goals

> Strategic objectives and metrics for the AI Employee to optimize towards.

## Mission Statement

To augment human productivity by autonomously handling routine tasks while ensuring full transparency, security, and human oversight for all significant decisions.

## Q1 2026 Goals

### Operational Efficiency
- [ ] Process 90% of routine emails within 4 hours
- [ ] Reduce manual file organization time by 80%
- [ ] Achieve < 5 minute response time for urgent keyword triggers
- [ ] Maintain 99% uptime for all watchers

### Financial Management
- [ ] Process all invoices within 24 hours of receipt
- [ ] Send payment reminders with 100% accuracy
- [ ] Flag 100% of unusual expenses for review
- [ ] Generate weekly financial summary reports

### Communication
- [ ] Respond to all important emails within SLA
- [ ] Zero missed urgent WhatsApp messages
- [ ] Maintain professional tone score > 90%

## Priority Matrix

### P0 - Critical (Immediate)
- Payment deadlines
- Legal document review
- Security alerts
- Customer complaints

### P1 - High (Same Day)
- Important emails (flagged)
- Invoice processing
- Urgent keyword messages
- Scheduled reports

### P2 - Medium (48 Hours)
- Regular correspondence
- File organization
- Non-urgent updates
- Weekly summaries

### P3 - Low (This Week)
- Archive maintenance
- Documentation updates
- Optimization tasks
- Nice-to-have features

## Success Criteria

The AI Employee is successful when:

1. **Reliability**: System runs 24/7 without human intervention for routine tasks
2. **Accuracy**: < 1% error rate on automated decisions
3. **Responsiveness**: Urgent items addressed within 5 minutes
4. **Transparency**: Every action logged and auditable
5. **Safety**: Zero unauthorized actions or security breaches
6. **Value**: Measurable time savings reported weekly

## Review Schedule

- **Daily**: Dashboard review, approval queue check
- **Weekly**: KPI review, error analysis
- **Monthly**: Goal progress, rule adjustments
- **Quarterly**: Full system audit, goal reset
